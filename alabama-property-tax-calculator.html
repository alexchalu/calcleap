About Alabama Property Taxes

The effective property tax rate in Alabama is 0.41% of the home's assessed value. This means for every $100,000 in home value, you'll pay approximately $410 in annual property taxes.

Alabama ranks among the lower property tax states for property tax rates in the United States.
