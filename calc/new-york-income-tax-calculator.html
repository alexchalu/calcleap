Frequently Asked Questions

Does NY tax Social Security?
No. New York does NOT tax Social Security benefits, regardless of income level.
Do I pay NYC tax if I work in NYC but live outside?
No. NYC tax is based on residency, not work location. Only residents of the 5 boroughs pay NYC tax.
When are NY estimated taxes due?
Quarterly: April 15, June 15, September 15, and January 15. Pay estimated tax if you owe $300+ after withholding.
