What Renters Insurance Covers

  • ✅ Personal Property: Damage or theft of your belongings (furniture, electronics, clothing)
  • ✅ Liability Protection: Legal fees if someone is injured in your rental
  • ✅ Additional Living Expenses: Hotel costs if your rental becomes uninhabitable
  • ✅ Medical Payments: Guest injuries regardless of fault

Average Renters Insurance Cost (2026)

National average: $15-$30/month or $180-$360/year for $30,000-$50,000 in coverage.

Renters insurance is one of the most affordable types of insurance, often costing less than $1 per day.

How to Lower Your Premium

  • 💰 Bundle with auto insurance for 10-25% discount
  • 💰 Increase your deductible (lowers monthly cost)
  • 💰 Install security systems, smoke detectors, deadbolts
  • 💰 Ask about loyalty, student, or military discounts
