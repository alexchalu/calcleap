Understanding Property Division Costs

Costs can vary widely based on:

  • Geographic location and local attorney rates
  • Case complexity and level of dispute
  • Court fees and filing costs
  • Expert witness fees (if needed)
  • Whether the case settles or goes to trial

Ways to Reduce Costs

  • Consider mediation before litigation
  • Organize documents and information in advance
  • Communicate efficiently with your attorney
  • Explore unbundled legal services for simpler cases
