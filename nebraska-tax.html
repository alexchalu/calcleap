❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Nebraska income tax rate in 2026?

Nebraska has a state income tax rate of 2.46-5.84% with 4 brackets. Graduated income tax.

What is the Nebraska sales tax rate?

The Nebraska state sales tax rate is 5.5%. However, cities and counties may add local sales taxes, so the total rate you pay could be higher. Always check the combined rate for your specific location.

How much is property tax in Nebraska?

The average effective property tax rate in Nebraska is 1.73% of your home's assessed value. For a $350,000 home, that's approximately $6,055 per year. Actual rates vary by county and municipality.

Is Nebraska a tax-friendly state?

Nebraska has moderate tax rates with income tax at 2.46-5.84%. Combined with 5.5% sales tax and 1.73% average property tax, the overall tax burden is reasonable compared to many states.
