Frequently Asked Questions

How is exact age calculated?

We calculate the difference between your birth date and today, accounting for varying month lengths and leap years to give you the precise age in years, months, and days.

What is my age in days?

Your total age in days is calculated from your birth date to today. A person who is 30 years old has lived approximately 10,957 days.

When is my next birthday?

The calculator shows how many days until your next birthday based on today's date.
