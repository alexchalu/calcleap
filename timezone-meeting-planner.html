About Timezone Meeting Planner

This free tool helps remote teams and international collaborators find meeting times that work across different time zones. Perfect for:

  • Remote team meetings
  • International client calls
  • Global webinars and conferences
  • Virtual events across continents

Tips for Scheduling Across Time Zones

  • Aim for 9am-5pm: Try to keep all participants within business hours
  • Rotate inconvenient times: Share the burden of early/late meetings
  • Use async communication: Not everything needs a meeting
  • Record meetings: For those in very inconvenient time zones
  • Consider daylight saving: Time zones can shift twice a year
