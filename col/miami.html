About Living in Miami

Cost of Living Index: 125 (national average = 100). Miami is 25% more expensive than the US average.

Housing: The biggest expense in Miami. Average 1BR apartment costs $2,200/month.

Transportation: Car recommended. Budget for gas, insurance, and parking.

Miami Living Costs Breakdown (2026)

  • Studio apartment: $1,700/month
  • 1 bedroom: $2,200/month
  • Groceries (1 person): $390/month
  • Transit pass: $110/month
  • Utilities: $135/month (electric, gas, water)

Tips for Living in Miami

  • Consider neighborhoods outside downtown for cheaper rent (20-40% savings)
  • Use public transit when possible - cheaper than car ownership
  • Cook at home - eating out costs 3-4x more than groceries
  • Look for roommates to split housing costs
